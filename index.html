How to Play?

  • Choose a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard. This will affect the game time, target size, and its movement speed.
  • Press the big "Start!" button to begin the round.
  • When the game starts, a red target will appear in the play area.
  • Your task is to click the target as quickly as possible.
  • After each hit, the target will instantly move to another random location.
  • Attention! On higher levels, if you don't hit the target fast enough, it will move on its own!
  • After the time runs out, you will see your score and the high score. Press "Play again?" to try and beat it!
